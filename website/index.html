Final Year Project

Interactive IoT-Based Comparative Study of Bridge Rectifier and Cockcroft-Walton Voltage Multiplier Circuits for Piezoelectric Energy Harvesting

This system harvests energy from piezoelectric sensors driven by mechanical vibration, then compares rectification topologies in real time using ESP32 master-slave control, relay switching, and cloud-connected analytics.

Piezoelectric Harvesting

Mechanical stress on piezo elements generates AC voltage. A vibration motor excites an acrylic platform; multiple piezo patches convert strain into electrical energy for downstream rectification.

Bridge vs CWVM

Bridge rectifiers convert AC to pulsating DC efficiently. Cockcroft-Walton multipliers stack voltage stages for higher output. This FYP measures which delivers the best usable DC for piezo harvesting.

System Flow
