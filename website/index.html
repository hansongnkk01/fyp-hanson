Final Year Project

Interactive IoT-Based Comparative Study of Bridge Rectifier and Cockcroft-Walton Voltage Multiplier Circuits for Piezoelectric Energy Harvesting

A single ESP32 controller measures two fixed circuits — Full-Wave Bridge Rectifier and 2-Stage Cockcroft-Walton — under identical vibration excitation. Each circuit is measured separately for 10 seconds at 1 Hz, with results streamed to this dashboard via Supabase.

Piezoelectric Harvesting

Mechanical stress on piezo elements generates AC voltage. A vibration motor (Relay 7) excites the platform during each 10 s capture window.

Two Circuits

Full-Wave Bridge converts AC to pulsating DC. 2-Stage CWVM multiplies voltage through two capacitor-diode stages. Only one circuit is active at a time.

Workflow

  1. Measure Full-Wave (button left)
  2. Measure 2-Stage CWVM (button right)
  3. Open Conclusion when both have data
